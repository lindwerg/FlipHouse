Four passes, fully automatic.

From a two-hour upload to postable shorts in a single run.

PASS 01

Find the moments

Transcript, audio energy and retention modeling locate the segments that actually travel — not random cuts.

PASS 02

Reframe to vertical

Speaker tracking keeps the right face centered as it moves, recomposing 16:9 into a clean 9:16 frame.

PASS 03

PASS 04
